Cesar Salad

Description

This is a delicous cesar salad recipe with store-bought dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of lettuce
  • 1/2 cup of cesar dressing
  • 1/2 cup of croutons

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Add the cesar dressing and toss to coat the lettuce evenly.
  3. Top with croutons before serving.
